AOL WHITELISTENING¶

URL : http://postmaster.aol.com/cgi-bin/wh…list_guides.pl

It’s very easy to get on their whitelist, but if they get tons of spam complains about your message, you will be removed to blacklist list very fast ^^

URL : http://help.yahoo.com/l/us/yahoo/mai…er/bulkv2.html http://help.yahoo.com/l/qe/snova/mail/postmaster/defer.html

https://help.yahoo.com/kb/postmaster/complaint-feedback-loop-sln3438.html It’s hard to get whitelisted on Yahoo, but give it a try.
